ABOUT US

HAVE YOU EVER WONDERED HOW AN AUTOMOBILE IS ABLE TO MOVE, THE OPERATION OF AN ENGINE OR THE WORKING MECHANISM OF A PLANETARY GEAR. THIS IS AN EDUCATIONAL WEBSITE TO ENABLE STUDENTS AND INDIVIDUALS UNDERSTAND THE WORKING PRINCIPLE OF THESE MECHANISMS.

MISSION

TO ENABLE STUDENTS UNDERSTAND THE WORKING PRINCIPLE OF CERTAIN MECHANISMS BY INTERACTING WITH A CAD FILE OF THE MECHANISM.

VISION

TO ENABLE STUDENTS BETTER UNDERSTAND AND APPRECIATE THE ENGINEERING THAT GOES INTO THE DESIGN OF THESE MECHANISMS AND CONTRIBUTE TO THE GLOBAL DEVELOPMENT.

ACHIEVEMENTS

BE OUR ACHIEVEMENT BY UPLOADING AND CONTRIBUTING TO THE COMMUNITY IN OUR QUEST TO RAISE UP THE NEXT GENERATION OF ENGINEERS.
